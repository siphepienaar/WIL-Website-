SOP 3: Acceptable Use Policy

← Back to Home

Objective

To establish acceptable standards for using ICT resources responsibly and securely.

Acceptable Use Rules

  • ICT resources are for academic and administrative purposes only.
  • Downloading illegal or unauthorized content is strictly prohibited.
  • Each user must maintain confidentiality of their credentials.
  • Devices must be regularly updated and virus-free.

Violation Management

  • 1st Offense: Written warning
  • 2nd Offense: Temporary suspension
  • 3rd Offense: Disciplinary action
