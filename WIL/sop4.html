SOP 4: Acquisition Procedures & Approvals

← Back to Home

Objective

To ensure IT acquisitions follow standardized, transparent, and cost-effective processes.

Procedure

  1. ICT Officer identifies required hardware/software.
  2. Request three vendor quotations for comparison.
  3. Procurement Officer reviews and approves purchase.
  4. Record items in the Campus Asset Register.
  5. System Administrator installs and verifies equipment.

Approval Hierarchy

ICT Officer → Procurement Officer → Management
