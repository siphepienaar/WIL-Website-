SOP 5: Help Desk Procedures

← Back to Home

Objective

To provide fast, efficient, and documented technical support for IT-related issues.

Help Desk Workflow

  1. User logs a ticket via email or the help desk portal.
  2. Help Desk assigns a ticket ID.
  3. Issue diagnosed and resolved or escalated.
  4. System Admin verifies and closes the ticket.

Response Time

  • Critical: within 4 hours
  • Standard: within 24 hours
  • Minor: within 48 hours
