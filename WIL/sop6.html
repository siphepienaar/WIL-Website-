SOP 6: Disaster Recovery Plan

← Back to Home

Objective

To ensure continuity of ICT operations in case of hardware, software, or network failure.

Preventive Measures

  • Automated daily backups (cloud & NAS).
  • UPS and surge protection for all critical servers.
  • Regular firmware updates and inspections.

Recovery Steps

  • Identify and isolate the failure.
  • Restore from verified backups.
  • Test systems for stability.
  • Document incident and update DR plan.
